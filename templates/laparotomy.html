Indication: Small bowel obstruction


Findings:

  • Closed loop small bowel obstruction in mid-ileum.
  • 30cm segment of bowel with 2 omental bands at either end.


Incision and Approach:

  • Midline incision.


Procedure:

  • Exploratory laparotomy performed.
  • Closed loop segment found and omental adhesions proximally and distally divided.
  • Segment viable, therefore no resection performed.
  • Small bowel walked from DJ flexure to terminal ileum - rest of small bowel healthy and no other abnormalities found.
  • 2 litres of normal saline washout to all four quadrants.


Closure:

  • 2x loop PDS for midline closure.
  • Clips to skin
  • Simple dressing.


Post-operative Instructions:

  • Routine post-operative observations
  • Analgesia
  • Mobilise as tolerated
  • Free fluids and build up diet as tolerated
  • Remove ryles tube
  • Prophylactic LMWH 6 hours post op
  • Clips out in 10-14 days post op

What is a common cause of small bowel obstruction?

  • Malignancy
